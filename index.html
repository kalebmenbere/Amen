About Holeta

Holeta is a vibrant town dedicated to the well-being and development of its children. This app, in collaboration with the Women and Children Office and Endalewgeta Children’s Village, aims to improve data collection, accessibility, and analysis for more effective interventions and resource allocation. Join us in making a difference for every child in Holeta!
